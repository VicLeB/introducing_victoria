Where I've Lived

Born in the USA but raised in Canada, I have spent most of my life growing up in the Greater Toronto Area. Then in 2020 I made the move to Hoboken, New Jersey to live with my Significant other. Together we recently relocated to Seattle, Washington and are enjoying life in the Pacific North West.

Education and Work

I attended University at Western University in Ontario, Canada where I earned my Bachelor of Science in Nursing. Following graduation, I worked in many hospital settings as a Registered Nurse in Canada and the USA.

Why tech?

Over the last year, I found myself curious about the tech world and in particular, software developement. Realizing the impact that technology has on people has motivated me to join an industry that is playing a large part in shaping our future. It is important for me to be challenged and continue to learn throughout the course of my career, making software engineering a great fit. Upon relocating to Seattle, I took some time to learn coding basics and found myself excited to learn again! Joining Flatiron School's Software Engineering Program is an exciting step on my journey!
